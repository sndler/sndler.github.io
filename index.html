Taiping Yao(姚太平)

I am a Researcher at Tencent Youtu Lab, where I work on computer vision and machine learning. And my research interests are face anti-spoofing, deepfake detection and adversial attack.

I got my bachelor's degree in Nanjing University (2012~2016) and master degree in Shanghai Jiaotong University (2016~2019).
